Registration is open until March 21, 2025 (please follow this link). The registration fee covers participation in all plenary sessions. In-person attendees will also have access to breakout discussions, as well as coffee and meal services. Registration fee is as follows

  • General attendees: $250
  • Early career attendees (Students and PhD received in 2020 or later): $150
  • Remote participants: $50

During registration, we strongly encourage all participants to submit a presentation (a title and abstract). Most will be assigned as posters, with a limited number selected for oral presentations. Given the collaborative nature of this workshop, ample time will be dedicated to group discussions aimed at developing research projects and proposals. Having a presentation from each participant will greatly enhance this process.

Travel Support

Travel support will be available for a limited number of early-career participants. The application period has closed. Due to unforeseen delays, the selection process is still underway, and notifications will be sent out as soon as possible. Thank you for your patience.
